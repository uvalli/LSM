About Us

Leytonstone Masjid has been serving the Leytonstone Muslim Community for over 40 years.

Today

The mosque has grown in size and can now accommodate about 1,000 people.

1976

Leytonstone Masjid opened  its doors to cater to the local Muslim community.

Pre 1976

Before Leytonstone Masjid

Prior to the Masjid's establishment, the Leytonstone Islamic Association was set up to serve the local Muslim population, and between 1969-1976 would do so by hiring out a hall, which would be used for prayers.

Collections then took place in order to fund the purchase of a property which would later be converted into a mosque.

In 1976, with donations entirely provided by the local Muslim community, a church was purchased, and subsequently converted into a mosque.
