Welcome to the Leytonstone Masjid

All Praise be to Allah, the most beneficent and the most merciful

Welcome to the Website of the Leytonstone Masjid and the Leytonstone Islamic Association (LIA). In these pages you should be able to find information about all the services we provide, forthcoming events, prayer times and much more.

For more information on the history of the Masjid and Association, please visit our “About Us” page.

We welcome all visitors to our Masjid and would gladly organise a tour upon request. Please use the Contact page to arrange this.

Please Donate generously to the Leytonstone Islamic Association. All proceed go towards the upkeep of the Masjid and helping us to serve the wider community. May Allah reward you greatly for all donations.
