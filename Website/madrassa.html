Madrassa

Timings:

Weekdays (Mon-Fri), 5pm-7pm

 

New Admissions:

New Admissions take place in September, subject to availability. Application forms can be downloaded online and must be completed, prior to starting Madrassa.

 

Fees:

£30 registration fee and then £25 per month, due on dates in Payment Schedule.

 

Student Resources and Equipment:

A list of essential student resources and equipment can be found in the syllabus. These will need to be purchased for each student and can be purchased from the Madrassa.

 

Examinations:

End of year examination dates can be found in the annual academic block timetable. These examinations are mostly carried out by external examiners. A mixture of written and oral examinations take place, and can be spread out over a one week period.
